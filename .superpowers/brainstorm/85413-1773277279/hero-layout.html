A. Overlapping Devices

iPhone in front, Android slightly behind and offset. Classic app showcase style. On scroll: iPhone slides up, Android slides down — creating a "splitting" parallax effect.

B. Side by Side

Devices next to each other with breathing room. On scroll: left device drifts down, right device drifts up — symmetric parallax divergence.

C. Staggered Heights

Devices at different vertical positions from the start — one high, one low. On scroll: they diverge further (one up, one down), amplifying the stagger into a dramatic split.

LCP Impact Note

The parallax scroll effect uses CSS transform: translateY() driven by a scroll listener — this is GPU-composited and does NOT affect LCP. The device images themselves are loaded with priority on Next.js Image, same as current hero. Net LCP impact: zero to minimal (one extra image load, but both can be priority-preloaded).
