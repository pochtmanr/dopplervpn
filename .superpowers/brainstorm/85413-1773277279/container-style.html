Device Container Style

Should the overlapping devices sit inside a card, or float freely in the hero space?

A. Inside Card

Keep the current glassmorphic card. Devices are contained, clean boundary. Parallax moves devices within (or slightly beyond) the card. Consistent with existing design language.

B. Floating (No Card)

Devices float directly in the hero with just a subtle glow behind them. More dramatic, more breathing room. Parallax feels unconstrained — devices can travel further on scroll. Modern, editorial feel.
